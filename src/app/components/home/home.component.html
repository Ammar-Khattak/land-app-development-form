SOMERSET COUNTY PLANNING BOARD

The Somerset County Land Development Review Resolution requires that certain proposed land development proposals be submitted to the Somerset County Planning Board for review and, where required, approval. Land development activities subject to County Planning Board approval include:

  • All subdivisions and resubdivisions of land within Somerset County.
  • All site plans for land development
  • Change of Use applications
